Since we opened in 2018 in Hong Kong, we've treat thousands of cats and dogs. We've expected our services into a full-service veterinary clinic, offering urgent care and routine care , like wellness exams, vaccines and spray, as well as surgeries, dental cleanings, and international health certificates.
This range not only benefits our clients,but also our medical team, for whom a broad case mix provides opportunities to learn, grow and practice at the top of their license. Diverse cases keep the team engaged and deepens their passion for veterinary medicine.
